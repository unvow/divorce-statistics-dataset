# CDC National Vital Statistics System (NVSS)

## Overview

The CDC's National Vital Statistics System is the primary source for U.S. divorce and marriage rate data. NVSS collects data from state vital registration offices.

## Data Used

- **Divorce rates by state** — crude divorce rate per 1,000 population
- **Marriage rates by state** — crude marriage rate per 1,000 population
- **National divorce rate trends** — annual crude rates from 2000 to 2023

## Notes

- Five states do not report divorce data to the CDC: California, Hawaii, Indiana, Minnesota, and New Mexico. National totals are based on 45 reporting states plus D.C.
- Historically, Georgia and Louisiana also did not report, but both now appear in NVSS state tables. Louisiana's anomalously low rate (0.9 per 1,000) may reflect incomplete reporting.
- Rates are "crude" rates based on total population, not married population.
- Data year: 2023 (most recent provisional data as of March 2026)
- The national divorce rate has declined 40% from 4.0 per 1,000 in 2000 to 2.4 per 1,000 in 2023.
